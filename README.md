With over 9 years of experience architecting high-performance mobile systems at scale. My career has been defined by a deep focus on **Swift**, **modular architecture**, and the intersection of **mobile software with hardware ecosystems**.

From leading core initiatives at **Microsoft** (Teams & Sensory Mixer) to modernizing legacy platforms, I specialize in transforming complex technical debt into scalable, testable, and maintainable SwiftUI/UIKit architectures.

---

### 🛠 What I’m Focused On:
* **Mobile Systems Architecture:** Designing modular, high-concurrency frameworks that power millions of daily active users.
* **Platform Modernization:** Leading the transition from Objective-C/UIKit to modern Swift Concurrency and SwiftUI patterns.
* **Founding Engineering:** Building 0-to-1 mobile experiences for AI-driven platforms and luxury marketplaces.
* **Engineering Velocity:** Optimizing CI/CD pipelines (GitHub Actions/Azure/Fastlane) to empower high-growth engineering teams.

---

### 📈 Beyond the Code
I am a strong advocate for **transparency in AI-assisted development** and believe the next generation of mobile software will be defined by how seamlessly we integrate LLMs into native user experiences. When I’m not shipping code, I’m mentoring senior engineers and refining the standards that drive engineering excellence.
